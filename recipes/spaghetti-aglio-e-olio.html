Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Description

This spaghetti is a staple in my repitoire. Feel free to play around with the heat levels until you find the right amount of spice for you. I always go back for seconds when this is the main dish. Best eaten fresh although it can be enjoyed after being in the fridge.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 1/2 to 1 cup past water
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin oliv oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 to 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp fresh lemon juice
  • 1/3 cup fresh parsley
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of slated water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions, cooking until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of hte pasta cooking water before draining the pasta.
  2. Heat the olive oil in alarge skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes. Stir and cook for 30 seconds to 1 minute, or until the garlic is lightly browned around the edges. Add salt, and several grinds of pepper.
  3. Add the spaghetti and toss to combine. Add 1/2 cup pasta water and the lemon juice and zest and toss. If pasta is seeming too dry, add the remaining 1/2 cup pasta water to create a light sauce.
  4. Season to taste. Garnish with parsley and serve with Parmesan.
